Tribute to Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

About Dr. Kalam

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He is popularly known as the “Missile Man of India” for his contribution to missile and nuclear programs.

Achievements

  • India's Missile Program (AGNI, PRITHVI)
  • Former President of India (2002–2007)
  • Bharat Ratna Awardee
  • Author of “Wings of Fire”
